Empanadas

Description

Empanadas are popular Latin American pastries made of dough filled with a variety of ingredients. The dough is typically made from flour, and the fillings can range from savory to sweet, with common choices like seasoned ground meat, cheese, or vegetables. Empanadas can be baked or fried until golden and crispy, and are often enjoyed as a snack or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Butter or oil
  • Ground beef or chicken (for filling)
  • Onions
  • Spices (cumin, paprika, etc.)
  • Eggs (for brushing)

Steps

  1. Make the Dough: Combine flour, water, and butter or oil to create a smooth dough. Let it rest, then roll it out.
  2. Prepare the Filling and Shape the Empanadas: Cook seasoned ground meat or other fillings. Cut the dough into circles, fill, fold, and crimp the edges.
  3. Cook the Empanadas: Bake in the oven or fry until golden and crispy. Serve hot with a dipping sauce of your choice.
